About Me

My name is Angad Singh, and I am currently pursuing a B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering at VIT Bhopal University.

I have a keen interest in web development and cloud computing, which has led me to create two websites: Notenest and Commerce Connect Central, an e-commerce platform.

My technical skills include HTML, CSS, Python, Java, C++, MongoDB, and MySQL. Additionally, I am a 5-star coder on HackerRank, showcasing my proficiency and dedication to coding and problem-solving.

Beyond academics, I am passionate about staying up-to-date with the latest technological trends and advancements. I enjoy working on challenging projects that push my boundaries and allow me to apply my knowledge practically.

I believe in continuous learning and am always looking for opportunities to expand my skill set and take on new challenges. My goal is to leverage my skills and passion to contribute to innovative solutions in the tech industry, making a meaningful impact through technology.

I am eager to collaborate with like-minded individuals and organizations to bring creative ideas to life.
